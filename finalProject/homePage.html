Welcome to Design

Here at design is everything, design is our lifestyle. We are a group of designers, producers, advertisers, students, and artists that all use design in our every day lives constantly. You can get and give feedback here for your own personal projects. If they are good enough and if you want you can submit your products to be seen as showcases in the home page or part of the gallery. If you like something from the gallery you can choose to buy the piece. Make sure to come back for weekly design tips and tricks to improve your designs!
