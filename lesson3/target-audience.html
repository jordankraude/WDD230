Target Audience

The target audience for this website includes anyone in the communinty of the chosen town. It focuses mainly towards buisnesses in the communinity or to those who are looking for information that has to do with the towns economy in some way shape or form (What events are happening in the town? What buisnesses are there? What things can we do here?).

Personas

Buisnesses in the Community Looking to Connect to Other Buisnesses

Citizens in the Community Looking For information

Scenarios

  • A buisness looking for a joint program with another
  • A buisness looking for opportunities for growth
  • Tourists looking for information about the town (town economy)
  • Citizens in the communinty looking for job opportunities
  • Buisnesses looking to help the community and help smaller buisnesses grow
